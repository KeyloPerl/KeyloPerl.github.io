Quick start

The Robot is equipped with an Intel NUC CPU that runs ubuntu 18.04 with preinstalled ROS melodic. (Now replaced with GEEKOM IT13 mini PC) The ultrasonic sensors are connected to the circuit board which contains the arm-M3 processor. The motors are controller by the ____ motor driver connected to arm processor board. The control commands are given to the arduino Leonardo and then to the arm processor. The arm processor controlls all of the interactions with the motor, LED strips and ultrasonic sensors. To turn on the Robot simply push the breaker up. Press the power button on the CPU to turn ON the Robot.
